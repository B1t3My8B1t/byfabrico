About Fabrico

Built on
Excellence


Founded in 2017, Fabrico is a high-end construction company known for delivering excellence in every project. Our journey began with small extensions and refurbishment jobs, and we have since expanded to specialise in new build homes across London and Berkshire.

Over the years we have grown to collaborate with developers and expanded into the homeowner market, aiming to deliver the highest quality results directly to our clients.

We pride ourselves on having a team of specialist trades who oversee every aspect of the project. Our rigorous sign-off process ensures quality at every stage. We've partnered with a leading construction software company, allowing clients to track every step of their project — from planning to completion — with full transparency.

"Delivering the highest quality results directly to our clients — at every stage, on every project."
